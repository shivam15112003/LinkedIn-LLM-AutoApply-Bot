JOB TITLE: Applied Ai Solutions Lead- Retail & Martech Innovation
Applied Ai Solutions Lead
About the job
About Us: 

We’re a leading gift registry company that’s transforming the way people celebrate life’s biggest moments. Our platform helps millions of users plan, share, and manage the most meaningful gifts from the people who matter most. We’re already a category leader—and now, we’re ready to level up with AI. As we enter our next chapter of growth, we’re creating a new AI Strategy & Innovation department to unlock new levels of personalization, user engagement, and intelligent automation across the business.
This is your opportunity to lead that effort.

Job description:

We’re looking for a visionary business leader with deep AI fluency to define and drive our AI agenda. This role is not about building models from scratch—we’ve got technical leadership for that. Instead, you’ll be the strategic brain behind how artificial intelligence can transform every corner of our customer experience and business operations.
You’ll work across departments to identify high-value opportunities for AI to create competitive advantage, then partner with our CTO and product teams to bring those opportunities to life.

What You’ll Do

Define a company-wide vision for AI that aligns with our mission, customer needs, and growth strategy
Lead the ideation and design of AI-powered solutions—such as:
Smart gift recommendations
Personalized registry setup flows
Predictive gift lifecycle nudges
AI-assisted customer service tools
Gifting trends and sentiment analysis
Partner with our CTO and engineering leads to assess feasibility, resource needs, and implementation timelines
Collaborate with marketing, design, product, and customer success teams to integrate AI into every stage of the user journey
Conduct market research on emerging AI technologies and frameworks; identify tools and vendors worth exploring
Own the AI innovation roadmap and help shape budget and resource allocation
Evangelize a data- and experimentation-driven mindset across the company
Represent the company externally as an AI-forward brand leader and internally as a thought partner to the executive team

What You'll Bring

7+ years in product strategy, business innovation, or AI-forward roles in consumer tech
Deep knowledge of the AI/ML landscape, including generative AI, recommendation engines, NLP, LLMs, and automation tools
Strong product instincts—you know how to connect AI capabilities to user behavior and unmet needs
Experience leading cross-functional innovation projects from concept to launch
Ability to evaluate vendor solutions, tools, and platforms from a strategic lens
Comfortable with ambiguity, rapid iteration, and agile decision-making
Outstanding communication and storytelling skills—you simplify complexity and bring people on the journey

Why Join Us

Join a mission-driven company reshaping how people celebrate life’s most meaningful moments
Own a high-impact, high-visibility role at the intersection of business and AI
Work with a seasoned leadership team that’s committed to innovation
Help build a brand-new function that will shape the future of gifting technology
